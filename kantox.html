FAQ.

How does Aura handle T+0 settlement?

By utilizing threshold signatures and atomic swaps, Aura locks capital in liquidity vaults simultaneously across both origin and destination corridors. The transfer is confirmed once 14 distributed nodes validate the cryptographic handshake. Finality is achieved in 140ms on average.

What institutional standards are audited?

Aura maintains SOC2 Type II, ISO 27001, and FIPS 140-2 compliance. Our smart contracts are formally verified by Runtime Verification and have completed a CBEST threat intelligence assessment with Tier-1 banks.

Can I connect existing Swift/Fedwire infrastructure?

Yes, the Gateway API provides native MT/MX message translation. Aura acts as a smart bridge translating Swift MX to on-chain instructions and vice‑versa, with full audit logging and reconciliation.
