Profile Information 👤

This is the profile panel. Here, users can view and edit their personal information, update their profile picture, and manage their bio.

Account Settings ⚙️

This is the settings panel. Users can change their password, manage email preferences, and configure two-factor authentication for enhanced security.

Notifications 🔔

This is the notifications panel. It displays recent activity, alerts, and messages. Users can customize which notifications they wish to receive.
